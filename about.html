Peak Escapes Backed By TravelManagers

Part of the House of Travel Group, TravelManagers Australia Pty Ltd is a network of over 550 home-based, mobile personal travel managers. Across the network, personal travel managers have on average over 20 years’ travel industry experience.

Since opening in New Zealand in 1986 and its arrival into Australia in 2007, House of Travel has grown into the largest independent travel company in Australasia.

This means that not only do we have access to amazing travel deals, but you have the security that your money is secure during the booking process. For details, please visit Peace of Mind with TravelManagers.

Travel With Confidence with
Our Consumer Guarantee

Our parent company, TravelManagers, is the first Australian travel business to set up a fully insured Travel Compensation Fund. This fund ensures that clients who have booked through us and are effected by a supplier going insolvent, will have costs outlaid protected. This situation is rarely covered by travel insurance so having this fund offers our clients increased protection.
